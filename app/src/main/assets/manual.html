Guidelines for using the Application

1. User Profile.

ParkMe user can register/store their(user) information and can update the information whenever required.

2. Parking Report.

Want to know your parking history? All you need to do is click on the parking report and it will show your all the saved parking history.

3. Parking Payment Receipt.

Whenever the user will select a parking spot, user needs to enter all his details and has to pay for the parking spot. As soon as the payment will be done, a payment receipt will be generated having all the details of the user as well as parking.

4. Parking Location.

Need to know your parking location? Just tap on Parking Location it will navigate you to the google maps and locate your parking location.

5. Help.

Got stuck? Need help? Contact us. Go to contact support and reach us.
